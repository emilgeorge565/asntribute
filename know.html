What is NFT?

NFT means non-fungible tokens (NFTs), which are generally created using the same type of technology used for making cryptocurrencies. In simple terms these computer assets are based on blockchain technology. They cannot be exchanged or traded equivalently like other cryptographic assets.

-> NFT is a digital asset that represents Internet collectibles like art, music, and games with an authentic certificate created by blockchain technology that underlies Cryptocurrency.

-> It is unique and cannot be forged or otherwise manipulated.

-> People can buy or exchange NFTs using cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin, Ethereum, etc. on certain sites.

What are NFTs used for?

People interested in Crypto-trading and people who like to collect artwork often use NFTs. Other than that, it has some other uses too like:

-> The most significant use of NFTs today is in digital content. Content creators see their profits enhanced by NFTs, as they power a creator economy where creators have the ownership of their content over to the platforms they use to publicize it.

-> NFTs have garnered considerable interest from game developers. NFTs can provide a lot of benefits to the players. With NFTs, developers can recoup their money by selling the items once they are finished with them.

-> NFTs provide domains with an easier-to-remember name. This works like a website domain name, making its IP address more memorable and valuable, usually based on length and relevance.

Pros of NFT-

NFTs have become popular nowadays due to their various benefits and value, some of its better aspects are:

-> They help in unlocking new revnue streams in gaming, arts, technology etc. and thus help in creating more jobs too.

-> As NFTs are based on blockchain technology, they are safe and secure and thus they can be traded easily without any fear of fraud.

-> NFTs are very accessible and people can use or access it from anywhere around the world and thus everyone can easily use or know more about them.

-> They are limited in number and thus owners have sense of proper ownership, and the ownership of each NFT can be verified using blockchain tech.

Cons of NFT-

Even though NFTs have lots of good aspect, it still has lots of negatives coming with the fact that it is a new technology.

-> As NFTs are part of an emerging technology, many of us are not quite familiar with it and thus it is difficult to understand for normal users.

-> NFTs are not regulated by any authority, and thus they are very risky to buy and sell and people can lose their investments if they don't properly understand NFTs.

-> NFT generation requires lots of energy, and thus it is not good for the environment or conservation of resources.

-> There are many cases of scams and frauds related to NFT technology, and thus people need to think twice before going into NFTs.
